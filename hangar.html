Learn it like a game

Build the ship. Fly the missions.

An AI agent is a spaceship: a reasoning core, robotic arms (tools), a cargo hold (context), fuel (token budget), shields (guardrails), and a hotline to mission control (escalation). Master each part in a mini-game, assemble them into a working ship, then fly missions where your choices — the same choices the exam tests — decide whether you dock or drift.

Six parts, ~1 minute each. Every part is one exam concept wearing a flight suit. Finish all six to unlock assembly.
